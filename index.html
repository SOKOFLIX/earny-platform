Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I have to buy the product first?

We operate a high-tier performance partnership. Providing free products to thousands of applicants results in a 90% drop-off rate. By offering the product at strict wholesale cost, we filter out casual applicants and partner only with serious creators.

How does the 40% revenue share work?

You earn a flat 40% of the net profit on every single sale generated through your unique tracking link or promo code. Depending on the brand campaign, this generally equates to R130 to R250 per unit sold.

When do I get paid?

To account for standard e-commerce return windows and chargebacks, commissions are held for a 30-day clearing period, after which they are disbursed directly to your nominated bank account or PayPal.

Can I run paid ads to my links?

Yes. Media buyers are welcome to run paid traffic on Meta or TikTok to their unique links. However, bidding on our branded search terms (e.g., Google Search Ads for "LGND clothing") is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.
